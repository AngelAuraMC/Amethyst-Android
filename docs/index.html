Why Copper

Everything you need to play

Built on the shoulders of giants — Amethyst and PojavLauncher — with extra polish and features.

⚙️

Full Java Edition

Play Minecraft: Java Edition from rd-132211 all the way up to the latest 1.21 snapshots and Combat Test versions.

🔧

Mod Support

Forge and Fabric supported out of the box. Install mods directly or via a headless installer for full modded gameplay.

🎨

Custom Themes

Copper ships with a fresh new theme and supports customization to make the launcher truly your own.

🚀

Performance Mods

Sodium and Krypton Wrapper are baked in for dramatically improved rendering performance on mobile hardware.

🖥️

Modern OpenJDK

Ships with OpenJDK 8, 17, and 21 — multi-architecture support for ARM32, ARM64, x86, and x86_64 devices.

🌍

Translated

Community-powered localization via Crowdin means Copper speaks your language, wherever you are.

🎮

Rewritten Controls

The entire controls system has been rebuilt from the ground up with native code, providing a responsive and natural feel.

🔊

OpenAL Audio

Full OpenAL audio support works on most Android devices, keeping your Minecraft experience immersive.

🔓

Free & Open Source

Licensed under GNU LGPLv3. Inspect, fork, and contribute — the code is yours to explore on GitHub.

Mod Management

Forge & Fabric, natively

Installing mods on mobile used to be painful. Copper integrates a full mod installer — both with and without GUI — so you can load up your favourite modpacks and get playing in minutes.

Forge Fabric Headless Installer Modrinth CurseForge

Performance

Play smooth on any device

Copper integrates Sodium, Krypton Wrapper, and MobileGlues rendering to squeeze every frame out of your Android hardware. Even older devices can experience buttery-smooth gameplay.

Sodium Krypton Wrapper GL4ES MobileGlues VirGL
